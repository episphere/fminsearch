Examples

You can put the equation in the text area below, it's URL, or you can select from the model drop down. For a one-click feeling of how fast does this in-browser does the stochastic descent converge, click this button. To follow regression step by step, open devtools console: slower, more complex functions, such as weibullCdfModel, will tipically report progress there. You can also follow regression step by step by setting number of iterations to a low value and then pressing "Fit" repeatedly. Note, however, that if that number is too low, for example 10, the non-linear regression may not have enough iterations to start converging.
